SSWAP Mediator

Align unknown cottage-booking ontologies on the fly. Step one analyses a remote RDG and stores the alignment. Step two reuses that alignment to invoke the service, transform the RRG, and render normalized booking suggestions.

Step 1 · Describe Remote Service

Provide the RDG endpoint (service description) and the invoke endpoint of the remote SSWAP service. The mediator downloads the RDG, auto-suggests matches, and lets you confirm or override every mapping.

Alignment Preview

Step 2 · Invoke Through Mediator

The mediator builds a RIG using the confirmed alignment, invokes the remote service, and transforms the RRG back into the native schema used by this UI.
